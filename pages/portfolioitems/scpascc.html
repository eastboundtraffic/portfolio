South Central PA Sickle Cell Council Nonprofit Site

Project Description

Client requested a new website to educate their clients and the public about Sickle Cell Disease

Project Details

  • Worked on a team of 5 to create the site
  • Used Axure to prototype the site and photoshop to create layouts for customer to approve
  • Primarily worked on Services, Programs, About Us, Board of Directors, and Testimonials pages
  • Utilizes modals and jquery tabs and collapsible boxes to reduce amount of content initially displayed on a page
  • Site can be found live at www.scpascc.org
